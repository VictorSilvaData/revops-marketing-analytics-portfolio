### Revenue Operations \& Marketing Analytics Portfolio

###### End-to-End Revenue Analytics Portfolio



This repository contains a collection of data analytics projects focused on Revenue Operations (RevOps), Marketing Performance, and Customer Analytics.

The goal of this portfolio is to demonstrate how data analysis can support decision-making across the entire revenue funnel — from marketing campaigns to customer retention.

The projects combine SQL-based data analysis with interactive dashboards to uncover insights and optimize business performance.



#### Revenue Analytics Framework

The projects follow a Revenue Operations framework covering the full customer lifecycle:



Marketing Campaigns

&#x20;       ↓

Lead Generation

&#x20;       ↓

Sales Pipeline

&#x20;       ↓

Revenue Growth

&#x20;       ↓

Customer Retention



Each project focuses on a different stage of this pipeline.

##### 02 — Lead Conversion Analysis

Focuses on the lead generation stage of the revenue funnel.

The analysis evaluates how leads progress through qualification stages and identifies the most effective lead sources.



###### Key topics:

* Lead conversion rates
* Lead source performance
* Cost per lead
* Lead qualification efficiency



##### 03 — Sales Pipeline Analysis

Examines how opportunities progress through the sales funnel.

The goal is to identify bottlenecks in the sales pipeline and improve deal conversion rates.



###### Key topics:

* Pipeline conversion rates
* Deal win rates
* Sales cycle length
* Pipeline stage performance



##### 04 — SaaS Revenue Analysis

Analyzes recurring revenue performance and growth metrics.



###### Key topics:

* Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
* Customer lifetime value
* Revenue growth trends
* Revenue by customer segment



##### 05 — Customer Churn Analysis

Focuses on customer retention and churn behavior.



###### Key topics:

* Customer churn rate
* Retention patterns
* Customer lifetime value
* Churn drivers



##### 06 — Salesforce CRM Analytics

Analyzes CRM data to understand how marketing and sales activities translate into revenue.



###### Key topics:

* Lead source ROI
* Sales performance
* CRM pipeline health
* Customer acquisition efficiency
