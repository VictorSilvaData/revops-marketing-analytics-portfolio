\# 📊 Lead Conversion \& Revenue Analysis



\## 🔍 Overview



This project analyzes the lead-to-customer conversion process in a simulated SaaS environment, focusing on channel performance, lead quality, and revenue generation.



The goal is to identify which acquisition channels generate the most valuable customers and how conversion efficiency impacts revenue outcomes.



\---



\## 🎯 Business Problem



Marketing teams generate a high volume of leads, but not all leads convert into customers.



Understanding:

\- Which channels generate high-quality leads

\- How efficiently leads convert

\- Where revenue is actually coming from



is critical to optimize marketing investment and improve business performance.



\---



\## ❓ Business Questions



\- Which channels generate the most leads?

\- Which channels have the highest conversion rates?

\- Which leads generate the most revenue?

\- What is the relationship between lead quality and revenue?

\- Where should the company invest more in acquisition?



\---



\## 🗂 Dataset



A simulated SaaS dataset was created to reflect real-world CRM scenarios, including:



\- Lead source (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn, Referral)

\- Company size and industry

\- Conversion behavior

\- Revenue generation

\- Conversion time



The dataset was designed to mimic real business dynamics such as:

\- Higher conversion rates from referrals

\- Higher revenue from enterprise customers

\- Lower quality leads from high-volume channels



\---



\## 🧠 Analysis



The analysis focuses on three key areas:



\### 1. Channel Performance

\- Lead volume by source

\- Revenue generation by channel



\### 2. Lead Quality

\- Classification into High, Medium, and Low value leads

\- Relationship between quality and revenue



\### 3. Conversion Efficiency

\- Conversion rate

\- Average time to convert



\---



\## 📊 Dashboard



The dashboard was built in Power BI and structured to support executive decision-making.



It includes:

\- KPI overview (Leads, Conversion Rate, Revenue, Revenue per Lead)

\- Channel performance analysis

\- Lead quality breakdown

\- Conversion behavior insights



\---



\## 💡 Key Insights



\- Referral and LinkedIn generate higher-quality leads with better revenue performance.

\- Facebook Ads drives high lead volume but lower conversion efficiency.

\- Enterprise leads generate higher revenue but require longer conversion time.

\- Not all lead volume translates into business value.



\---



\## 🚀 Business Recommendations



\- Increase investment in high-performing channels such as Referral and LinkedIn.

\- Optimize or reduce spend on low-conversion channels like Facebook Ads.

\- Implement lead scoring to prioritize high-value prospects.

\- Improve nurturing strategies for leads with longer conversion cycles.
